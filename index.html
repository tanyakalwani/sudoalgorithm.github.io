Introducing Smartspot


Smartspot is a community based platform that connects people who want to rent their mobile data with people who are looking for internet connectivity.

Provider


  • Flexibility to choose the amount of data you wish to rent and the time period of its rental.

  • Rent your mobile internet with the community without your password being visible, securely and safely.

  • Receive payment for the data used and time for which you kept the connection ongoing.

User


  • See a list of all the nearby providers, with the amount of data available for use, and the time duration for which it will be available.

  • Connect to a connection, without the hassle of authentication, with just a click.
